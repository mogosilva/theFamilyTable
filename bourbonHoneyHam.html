BOURBON HONEY HAM

View as Grocery List?

INGREDIENTS

  • 2/3 cup bourbon or whiskey
  • 1 cup clover honey
  • 1/3 cup molasses
  • 1/2 cup 100% fruit orange marmalade
  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 (5 pound) whole bone-in smoked ham, fully cooked, unsliced
  • 1/8 cup whole cloves
  • GROCERY LIST COMPLETE!

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F°
  2. In a medium saucepan, heat bourbon, honey, molasses, and marmalade over medium heat for 30 minutes or until reduced by half
  3. Set aside
  4. With a sharp knife, cut a diamond pattern on the fatty part of the ham
  5. Stud whole cloves in each diamond, at points where lines cross.
  6. Spread half of the bourbon glaze over the ham
  7. Roast ham for 30 minutes, uncovered
  8. Baste occasionally with remaining glaze and continue to roast for another 15 minutes
  9. Let ham stand for 20 minutes before slicing
